# 21.1 MAJOR CEREAL DISEASES

## 🌾 **1. Ergot (Claviceps purpurea)**
**Crop:** Maize, sorghum (rare), millet (rare)  
**Symptoms:**  
- Purple to black sclerotia replacing seeds  
- Toxic alkaloids harmful to livestock  
- Reduced grain quality  
- Contamination risks

**Recommended Jubaili Products:**
- **FungiCare** – broad-spectrum fungicide highly effective against fungal seed diseases  
- **FunguForce Gold** – systemic fungicide with excellent control of Claviceps-type pathogens  
- **TotalForce** – strong wide-range fungicide for cereal fungal infections  

**Notes:**  
- Apply before flowering for best effect  
- Severe cases require destroying infected heads
